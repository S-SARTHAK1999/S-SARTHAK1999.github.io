Research Intern – Dual-Arm Robot Modelling

Short research internship focused on modelling a dual-arm robotic system for collaborative manipulation tasks.

  • Developed a 4-DOF dual-arm model in Simscape Multibody and validated motion in SolidWorks.
  • Derived forward kinematics using Denavit–Hartenberg parameters.
  • Used Python to compare analytical kinematics with simulation results.
