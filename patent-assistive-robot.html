Assistive Robotic System for users with autism spectrum disorder

Indian Patent Application No. 202531062319 ¬∑ Filed June 2025

This patent describes a dual-arm mobile social robot designed to support children with autism spectrum disorder in developing social and communication skills.

  • Designed a wheeled base with a modular torso and two articulated arms for expressive gestures.
  • Defined interaction routines combining facial expressions, speech, and gamified tasks.
  • Integrated sensors for user tracking and safe operation in indoor environments.
  • Evaluated engagement and responsiveness with pilot user studies.

The system targets low-cost deployment in clinics and schools, with easy customisation of activities by therapists.
