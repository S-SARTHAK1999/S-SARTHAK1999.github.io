Design, Fabrication and Control of a Cable-Driven Parallel Robot

Dhruv Sorathiya, Sarthak Sahoo, Vivek Natarajan ¬∑ 10th Indian Control Conference (ICC 2024)

This work presents the complete pipeline of building a planar cable-driven parallel robot (CDPR) from scratch and experimentally validating its motion control.

  • Formulated kinematics and dynamics for an underactuated CDPR with three cables.
  • Designed the robot structure and winch assembly in SolidWorks and fabricated the setup using aluminum profiles and 3D-printed fixtures.
  • Performed motor sizing for low-speed smooth operation and integrated encoders for feedback.
  • Implemented trajectory-tracking controllers in MATLAB/Simulink and validated them on the physical platform.

The resulting platform continues to be used in the CDC Lab for experimental studies on nonlinear control and trajectory planning.
