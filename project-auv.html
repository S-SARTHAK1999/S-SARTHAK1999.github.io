Autonomous Underwater Vehicle – Design & Hydrodynamics

As part of Team Tiburon, I worked on the mechanical design and analysis of a modular AUV platform.

  • Designed the main frame to house eight thrusters for full 6-DOF control and payload capacity.
  • Performed structural FEA using ANSYS to ensure safety against hydrostatic and handling loads.
  • Studied hydrodynamic drag and current effects to inform controller design.
