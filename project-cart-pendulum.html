Flatness-Based Control of a Cart–Pendulum System

This project explores nonlinear modelling and flatness-based control for a cart–pendulum system.

  • Derived Lagrangian dynamics and obtained state-space representation for both inverted and non-inverted modes.
  • Implemented linear controllers (pole placement and LQR) around upright equilibrium.
  • Currently constructing flatness-based rest-to-rest trajectories for the non-inverted pendulum and studying robustness to disturbances.
