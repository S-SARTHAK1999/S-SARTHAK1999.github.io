Project Research Intern – Cable-Driven Parallel Robot

During this internship, I built the experimental CDPR platform that later formed the core of our ICC 2024 work.

  • Designed the full robot structure, winch drums, and cable routing in SolidWorks.
  • Selected motors and gearboxes based on required tension and acceleration profiles.
  • Fabricated the frame, routed Dyneema cables, and tuned pre-tension to avoid slack.
  • Implemented motion control experiments using Arduino, MATLAB, and Python for DAQ.
