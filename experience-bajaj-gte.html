Graduate Trainee Engineer – Manufacturing Engineering (Engine)

I worked on improving reliability and productivity of engine assembly stations on Pulsar lines.

  • Optimised fuel leak-test cycle time from 21 s to 17 s by adjusting pneumatic logic and pressure-settling steps.
  • Documented lessons learned in standard operating procedures for future projects.
